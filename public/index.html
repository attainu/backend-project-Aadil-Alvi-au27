Legacy

Started in 2013 with the goal to serve the right combination of ingredients in the appropriate quantity in order to produce the finest cuisines in an authentic environment and service.

Smoke'N Grills a brain child of the third generation Mr. Faizan Kotwala- son of Mr. Javed Kotwala Managing Director-Smoke'N Grills and grandson of Mr. Abubakkar (Abubhai) founders of Smoke'N Grills.

Ingredients are essential to the flavor of any platter, and we always preserve the taste with the appropriate quantity and quality. As the Khansamas (Chef) belong to the dynasty of the Moghuls the taste and flavours are traditional and authentic.

Smoke'N Grills began with new thoughts and ideas which made it a pioneer in various food specialities including Mughlai, Punjabi, Chinese, Sea Food cuisines and not the least Raan. Being awarded the title of "Raan King" this dish can be cooked in 10 different variants. The restaurant is split between the Darbar Hall and the Galeecha in two lovely parts.
